  • ○ Linear motion

3. Human voice is produced by vibrations in?
  • ○ Vocal cords
  • ○ Tongue
  • ○ Lips
  • ○ Lungs
Answer: Vocal cords
4. Can sound be produced without vibration?
  • ○ No
  • ○ Yes
  • ○ Sometimes

  • ○ Parallel to wave direction

  • ○ Longitudinal
  • ○ Transverse
